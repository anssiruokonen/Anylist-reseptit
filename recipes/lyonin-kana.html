Lyonin Kana (Chicken Lyon Style)

A classic French-inspired dish featuring tender chicken in a creamy mushroom and onion sauce.

Prep: 20 min
Cook: 45 min
Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 600g chicken thighs, boneless
  • 200g button mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 large onions, thinly sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 200ml heavy cream
  • 100ml dry white wine
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 2 tsp fresh thyme leaves
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Season chicken with salt, pepper, and paprika
  2. Heat oil in skillet, sear chicken 4-5 min per side until golden. Set aside.
  3. Add butter, cook onions 10-12 min until caramelized
  4. Add mushrooms and garlic, cook 5-6 min
  5. Pour in wine, scrape up browned bits, reduce by half
  6. Stir in cream and mustard, add thyme, simmer
  7. Return chicken to pan, cover and cook 15-20 min until done (74°C)
  8. Season to taste, garnish with parsley. Serve with rice or bread.
